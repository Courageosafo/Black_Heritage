Elimina Castle, nestled along the Ghanaian coast, is a poignant symbol of the region's colonial history Originally constructed by the Portuguese in the 15th century, the castle changed hands betwee various European powers over the years,witnessing the transatlantic slave trade.Today, it serves as a museum,allowing visitors to delve into the complex and impactful history that unfolded within its walls.
